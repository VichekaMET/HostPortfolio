About Me

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer

My name is Met Sereyvicheka, born on November 16, 2002, in Ratanakiri Province, Cambodia. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Energy Engineering from the Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC), where I specialized in Electronic Engineering and Automation. I am currently continuing my studies in France at Toulouse INP – ENSEEIHT, pursuing a Master’s in Electronic Systems for Embedded and Communicating Applications (ESECA). Over the past years, I have worked deeply with embedded systems development, IoT hardware and firmware, power electronics, motor control, and PCB design. I enjoy building efficient systems, solving engineering challenges, and contributing to innovative technological solutions. I have led multiple engineering projects, including robotic systems, LoRaWAN communication devices, and multi-motor ESC design. My goal is to become a highly skilled embedded systems engineer capable of developing reliable, optimized, and intelligent electronic systems that make a real impact. Beyond engineering, I value teamwork, continuous learning, and exploring new ideas that push the limits of technology and creativity.
